Parotta Glossary

Veechu Parotta
Thin, flaky parotta made by rapid hand-spinning technique.
Kothu Parotta
Shredded parotta stir-fried with egg, onions & spices.
Kizhi Parotta
Parotta steamed in a banana leaf parcel with curry filling.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What types of Parottas do you serve?

We serve over 20 varieties, including Plain, Egg, Kothu, Chilli, Veetu, Nool, Bun, Kizhi, Paal, Wheat, Lappa, and Malabar Parottas.

Q2: What are your opening hours?

Open every day from 5:00 PM to 11:00 PM.

Q3: Do you provide home delivery?

Yes! We deliver via Swiggy, Zomato, and direct phone orders within a 5 km radius.

Q4: Can I pre-order for a large group or party?

Absolutely! Please contact us at least 2 hours in advance for bulk or custom orders.

Q5: Are your items halal and hygienic?

Yes. All items are prepared fresh daily with halal-certified ingredients in a hygienic kitchen.
